HISTORY

Inspired by the Gandhian philosophy of Gram Swaraj, or 'self-rule', a group of residents of Dimili village (Kotturu Mandal, Srikakulam District) and other area social activists founded the Bapuji Rural Enlightenment and Development Society (BREDS) in 1983.
